More about Precision Auto Care


Precision Auto Garage has been serving the Nairobi community for over 10 years, specializing in premium vehicle care and expert diagnostics. Located in Karen road, we've built a reputation for excellence in automotive service.


Our team of highly skilled technicians uses state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and genuine parts to ensure your vehicle receives the best possible care. Whether it's routine servicing, major repairs, or specialized diagnostics, our team is equipped to handle all your automotive needs with professionalism and precision
